About

The objective of establishing "Bela Bhawani Institute of Technology" is to provide quality software, hardware, and networking training to students of all age groups who are eager to pursue professional courses in computing to advance their careers. The purpose of the academy is to cultivate true IT professionals. Bela Bhawani Institute of Technology is one of the most reputed educational institutions in Narora. The Institute of Computer Education and Technology offers high-quality computer education to students. The diverse range of IT courses includes ADCA, Nielit-CCC, Tally, Python, DCA, and Web Technology.

Vision & mission

Welcome to our Computer Center! Our institute serves as a comprehensive technology provider for students, researchers, and the teaching community, addressing their diverse computational, technological, and internet needs. Our goal is to provide a strong foundation in programming that will support their academic and professional growth. In addition to this foundational training, the Computer Center offers advanced resources and support for research and development activities. We are committed to fostering an environment that encourages innovation, creativity, and collaboration among all members of our community. The Center also plays a crucial role in maintaining and enhancing the institute's technological infrastructure, ensuring seamless connectivity and robust computational capabilities. We strive to provide world-class networking facilities, including secure Wi-Fi, video conferencing, and email services, to support the institute's academic and administrative functions. Our web development team is dedicated to designing, developing, and maintaining the institute's online presence, ensuring it reflects our commitment to excellence. Additionally, we develop customized software solutions to meet the specific needs of the institute and its stakeholders. To stay ahead in the ever-evolving tech landscape, we regularly conduct short-term courses and training sessions for working professionals, teachers, and students, helping them enhance their skills in emerging areas. This Center is currently headed by Mr. Gaurav Kumar Verma.
